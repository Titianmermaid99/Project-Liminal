# Purpose

This document serves as the creative incubator for Project Liminal.

It contains ideas that have **not** been approved for implementation.

Recording ideas here ensures they are preserved, discussed, and evaluated without affecting the current design documented in the Game Bible.

Ideas remain here until they are either:

- Approved and moved into the Game Bible.
- Rejected.
- Archived.
